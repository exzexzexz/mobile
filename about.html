ABOUT


EXZ is a Brazilian musician who fuses electronic based rhythms with psychedelic tone colours and melodies.
Alexandre Silveira started to release and perform his own music in São Paulo where he found a vibrant and growing music scene. At first with Voodoohop where he could explore lower tempos and later on with Mamba Negra where he started to experiment with more electronic elements and faster bpms. Currently living in Berlin EXZ is performing a distinctive live act that creates a journey through different atmospheres and emotions.
He has already performed in different festivals and clubs around the world such as Fusion Festival in Germany, Garbicz Festival in Poland, Festival Nomade in Chile, Dekmantel Festival in Brazil, Kater Blau and Sisyphos in Berlin, Public Works in San Francisco among others.
